What Can You Do Now?
CAR ACCIDENT:

1) Turn off car ignitions if possible.

2) Alert others by putting on vehicle hazard lights and use a warning triangle if there is one available.

3) Keep the casualties warm and calm and help them to contact next of kin

CARDIAC ARREST:

1) Get an AED if possible

2) Repeat continuous cycles of 30 chest compressions and 2 ventilations

3) Use AED if available and follow its instructions

FIRE:

1) Alert others to the fire by shouting

2) Evacuate the area via exit stairs immediately

3) Fight the fire only if you are able to and without endangering yourself and others. Use a fire extinguisher or hose reel.
